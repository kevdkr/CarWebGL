Car Controls

  • Press UP arrow to move forward
  • Press DOWN arrow to move backward
  • Press and hold LEFT or RIGHT to rotate the tires
  • Press SPACE to stop the car

Camera Controls

  • Press 1 to switch to free camera

  • On Camera 1:

    • Press Q to zoom the lens out
    • Press W to zoom the lens in
    • Press S to move the camera in, or "dolly zoom"
    • Press A to move the camera out, or "dolly out"
    • Press F to make the camera follow the car
    • Press R to reset the position and zoom of Camera 1

  • Press 2 to switch to first-person view On Camera 2: Press and hold Z to swivel your head right Press and hold X to swivel your head left

  • Press 3 to switch to chase camera

Light Controls

  • Press 0 to turn overhead light on/off
  • Press 8 to turn siren lights on/off
  • Press 9 to turn headlights on/off
